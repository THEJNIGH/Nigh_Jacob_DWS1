History

The history of the hookah dates back to as far back as the 1560's. At this time tobacco was know to cause health issues. Thus, the water pipe was born. The hookah was to purify the harming effects that it did on the human body. It was also well known with the indian aristocrats and gentry.

"Oh! hookah of the magic bowl, Thou dost bring me greatest pleasure, Who likes not thee, hath not a soul, And can know of joy no measure.
Thy fragrance brings me visions bright-- Dispels the shadows of the night." From the book Medical Sentinel.

Instructions

These are the instructions to put the hookah together and how to get a session going

  1. Take base and fill with enough water to where the stem is submerged by an inch.
  2. Take stem and place on top of the base and make sure the seal between steam and base is tight.
  3. Take the ash pan and place it on top of the stem.
  4. Get natural coals.
  5. Place coals on a burner.
  6. Grab the head to the hookah.
  7. Find some shisha here are some flavors you can choose from:
    • Blueberry Mint
    • Orange
    • Double Apple
    • Mint
    • Guava
    • Key Lime Pie
    • Chocolate Mint
  8. Take shisha and spread into the head of the hookah.
  9. Place foil over the head.
  10. Poke push pen sized holes all over head.
  11. Place finished head on top of the grommet of the hookah.
  12. When coals are red hot all over place coals on top of head
  13. Put in hose
  14. Enjoy!
