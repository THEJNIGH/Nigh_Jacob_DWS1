Hookah and the Origin

The history of the hookah dates back to as far back as the 1560's. At this time tobacco was know to cause health issues. Thus, the water pipe was born. The hookah was to purify the harming effects that it did on the human body. It was also well known with the indian aristocrats and gentry.
